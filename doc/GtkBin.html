GtkBin — A container with just one child

Synopsis

The module GtkBin is not used directly but its methods can be used by its child modules. Below is an example using a GtkButton which is a direct descendant of GtkBin. Here it is also clear that a button is also a container which in principle can hold anything but by default it holds a label. The method gtk-container-add() comes from GtkContainer and get-child() comes from GtkBin.

my GTK::V3::Gtk::GtkLabel $label .= new(:label<pqr>);
my GTK::V3::Gtk::GtkButton $button .= new(:empty);
$button.gtk-container-add($label);

$l($button2.get-child);
is $l.get-text, 'pqr', 'text label from button 2';

Of course, it is easier to do the next

my GTK::V3::Gtk::GtkButton $button .= new(:label<pqr>);
